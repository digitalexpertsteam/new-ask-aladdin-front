EGYPT AND SAFETY

Egypt has always been a favorite destination for tourists from all over the world. Egypt is, and always will be, a safe country to visit. Tourists from all over the world will always feel safe in Egypt more than any other country in Europe, or in America. Egyptian people have always been known for their wonderful hospitality and generosity with tourists. "We are always friendly toward foreigners and they are very important to us. We would defend them with our own lives. They are our guests, and we always consider our guests as family members". This is a quote from a taxi driver recently from an interview on Egyptian Television.
